Transcription

SECTION 1

You will hear a telephone conversation between a man who is looking for a part-time job for his son and the manager of a garden centre.

[Manager]
Greenwell Garden Centres. Station Road branch. How can I help?

[Man]
Oh, hello. I'm calling about the part-time job you were advertising last week... for a general assistant. My son's looking for a part-time job...

[Manager]
Well, we'll shortly have a position available in our new Market Road branch... or there's one available right now in our branch in Station Road...

[Man]
It's definitely the current job I'm calling about.

[Manager]
Now there were a couple of options regarding working hours... What about Sunday? That'd work, provided he could do a full day.

[Man]
Great. And what would the job involve exactly?

[Manager]
Working on the checkout... a lot of lifting of some quite heavy items... assisting with customer enquiries... the plants need a lot of watering.

[Manager]
The first one being communication skills are important... some ability in maths, a basic level at least.

[Man]
So am I right in thinking he's still at high school?... Could we make it later instead, perhaps at 5?

[Manager]
Five would be better for us... My name's Maria Rapana... ring me on my direct line... 0213033874... I need to see Liam's passport at the interview...

That is the end of section one. You now have half a minute to check your answers.
